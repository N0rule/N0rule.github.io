Games

Introducing "Zombie Shooter", survival game created using Unity. In this game, players are tasked with fighting off hordes of zombies using a variety of weapons, including a pistol, shotgun, rifle, and sniper rifle. Each weapon has its own unique gimmick, adding an extra layer of strategy and excitement to the gameplay.

Pistol, for example, is perfect for taking out zombies at close range, while the shotgun is ideal for clearing out large groups of enemies. The rifle has laser to help with aim, and can be used in both close and long-range combat, sniper rifle allows players to take out zombies from a safe distance.

In addition to the weapons, players can also use various items, such as health packs and ammo, to aid in their survival.
