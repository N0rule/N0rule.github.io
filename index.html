Games

Presenting "Zombie Shooter", a survival game developed using Unity. Players are challenged to fend off waves of zombies with a selection of weapons, including a pistol, shotgun, rifle, and sniper rifle. Each weapon possesses its own unique feature, adding a new dimension of strategy and thrill to the gameplay experience.

For instance, the pistol is ideal for dispatching zombies at close range, while the shotgun excels at taking out large groups of enemies. The rifle, equipped with a laser for improved accuracy, can be utilized both in close and long-range battles. The sniper rifle, on the other hand, provides players with a safe means of eliminating zombies from a distance.

In addition to weapons, players have access to a range of items, such as health packs and ammunition, to enhance their chances of survival.

Cloud Hosting Solutions

I've had the privilege of utilizing Oracle Cloud for hosting various projects. One of my most notable achievements has been the establishment and management of a Discord bot and a Minecraft server.

To set up the Discord bot, I created a virtual machine on Oracle Cloud and installed the necessary software and dependencies. I then configured the bot's settings to cater to the specific needs of my community, and set up a MongoDB database to store user information and other data for the bot.

I also established a Minecraft Purpur server using Oracle Cloud. This involved creating a virtual machine, installing the necessary software and dependencies, and configuring the server settings to match the needs of my community. Additionally, I installed various plugins to improve the gameplay experience for my players. Hosting the Minecraft server on Oracle Cloud provided a valuable learning experience, as it showcased the platform's flexibility and scalability.
